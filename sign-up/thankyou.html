About the Author

Growing up in the 1980s molded my fascination with storytelling. Movies like The Goonies and The Princess Bride were tape-recorded and replayed repeatedly. I can recite each character's lines, word for word. I had journals filled with short stories. I have been writing for as long as I can remember.

Although I have a couple of books in several genres, I lean towards writing more urban fantasy than any other genre. My stories are always full of intense family drama, ancient magic, and world-altering chaos.

When I'm not writing fiction, I run 5Ks, lift heavy weights in my CrossFit gym, write poetry, and code JavaScript. Did I mention that I'm also a programmer? Well, that's a story for another time.

If you visit me on social media, you will find hundreds of photos of my two cats, Goldie and Gato. They are living proof that some love-hate relationships are absolutely vital.
